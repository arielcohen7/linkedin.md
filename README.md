## Voice

> The algorithm rewards a voice. It's not mine.

I'm not optimizing for what LinkedIn pays for.

I'm optimizing for what I'd say at 11pm to a friend who ships product for a living.

### Rules

- No **"Here's the thing."** Delete on sight.
- No **"Not X, it's Y"** flips. Once per post max. Zero is better.
- No motivational fluff. If a sentence could go on a coffee mug, it's out.
- No escalating triplets. Three items building to a climax = constructed.
- Hebrew when I mean Hebrew. Not for reach. For reciprocity.
- One per post: a real number, a named situation, or a concrete decision. No *"I learned"* without what it cost.

### The test

```text
Read the draft aloud.
If it sounds built: rewrite.
If it sounds said: ship.
```

About 60% of my drafts fail it. That's the point of writing it down.
